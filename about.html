Rajniti Samachar Kya Hai?

Rajniti Samachar ek free AI-powered political news tool hai jo Indian politics ki latest khabrein Hindi aur English dono mein deta hai. Hum Google Gemini AI ka use karke web se real-time news fetch karte hain aur use readable format mein present karte hain.

Hamara maqsad hai ki political news sirf English walon tak seemit na rahe — har Hindi bolne wala bhi aasani se apni rajneeti samajh sake.

Hum Kya Dete Hain?

Koi bhi political topic par AI se latest news mangwaao — BJP, Congress, Modi, state politics, elections — sab kuch. News Hindi aur English dono mein milti hai. Ek click mein professional PDF report bhi download kar sakte ho.

Technology

Rajniti Samachar Google Gemini AI model use karta hai jo real-time web search karke latest news laata hai. Ye ek simple free website hai — koi login nahi, koi subscription nahi.

Disclaimer

Yahan di gayi news AI-generated hai aur publicly available web sources pe based hai. Ye kisi bhi political party ki official position nahi hai. Hamesha original sources se verify karein. Hamaara kisi bhi party se koi sambandh nahi hai.
