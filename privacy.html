Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2025

1. Kaunsa Data Collect Hota Hai?

Rajniti Samachar aapka koi bhi personal data store nahi karta. Aap jo search topic daalte ho wo seedha AI (Google Gemini) ko jaata hai aur response ke baad hum kuch save nahi karte.

2. Google AdSense

Hamari website Google AdSense ads use karti hai. Google apne ads show karne ke liye cookies use karta hai jo aapki browsing activity track kar sakti hain. Aap Google ke Privacy Policy ko yahan padh sakte hain.

Aap Google ke ad personalization ko yahan manage kar sakte hain.

3. Google Gemini AI

Aapki news search queries Google Gemini API ke through process hoti hain. In queries ka use news fetch karne ke liye hota hai. Google ki privacy policy apply hoti hai. Hum aapki queries apne servers pe store nahi karte.

4. Cookies

Hum khud koi cookies set nahi karte. Google AdSense aur Google Fonts apni cookies set kar sakte hain. Aap apne browser settings se cookies manage kar sakte hain.

5. Third Party Links

Hamari website ke links doosri websites par le ja sakte hain. Un websites ki privacy policies hamaari jimmedari nahi hai.

6. Children

Yeh website 13 saal se kam umra ke bachon ke liye nahi hai. Hum jaante hue 13 se kam umra ke users se koi data collect nahi karte.

7. Changes

Hum is privacy policy ko kabhi bhi update kar sakte hain. Changes is page pe publish honge.
